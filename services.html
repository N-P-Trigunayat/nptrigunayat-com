All FAQs →
Do you work with clients outside Lucknow?
▾
Absolutely. We work with clients across India and internationally — the full project lifecycle is handled remotely over video calls, WhatsApp, and shared previews. Being based in Lucknow is a cost advantage we pass on to you, not a geographic restriction.
How do you handle projects where I have no technical knowledge?
▾
Most of our clients aren't technical — and that's exactly who we build for. We translate everything into plain language, explain trade-offs clearly, and make all decisions transparent. You'll never feel lost or patronised. We guide you from the first call to the final handover.
What if I need both a website and SEO?
▾
That's our sweet spot. We bundle web development and SEO together at a combined rate — and the advantage is that the site is built SEO-ready from the ground up, not retrofitted after the fact. Ask us about bundle pricing when you book your free consultation.
What does "fixed price" actually mean — can costs ever change?
▾
Fixed means fixed. We invest heavily in the scoping phase so our proposals are accurate. The price you approve is the price you pay — not a rupee more. The only exception is if you add significant scope after approval, which we flag in writing before proceeding.
How do I get started?
▾
Hit "Let's Talk", fill in a few lines about what you need, and we'll set up a free 30-minute discovery call. No commitment, no sales pressure — just a clear conversation about whether we're the right fit for your project.
