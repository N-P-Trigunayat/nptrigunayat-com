STORY
The Story

I BUILT THIS
FROM SCRATCH.
LITERALLY.

I didn't inherit a client list. I didn't launch with investors or a team. I started NP Trigunayat Systems in 2020 from Lucknow with an old desktop, a deep obsession with building things that work, and a conviction that good businesses deserve better than generic, copy-paste websites.

What I saw in the market frustrated me. Business owners were paying agencies lakhs of rupees and getting cookie-cutter WordPress sites that loaded slowly, looked like every competitor, and came with zero post-launch support. They deserved better.

So I built something different — a studio where you work directly with the developer. Where every line of code is written with your specific business goals in mind. Where I'm not managing 20 clients at once and rushing through yours.

Five years later, 50+ projects delivered across e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, SaaS, and F&B — the thing I'm most proud of isn't any single project. It's that nearly every client has come back for more, or referred someone new. That's the only metric that matters.

Understand Before You Build

I read your brief multiple times. I ask the questions that seem obvious. I want to know your customer, your competition, and your actual goal — not just what you said, but why you want it.

02

Every decision has a reason I can explain in plain English. You always know what's happening and why — no jargon shields, no smoke screens, no black boxes.
