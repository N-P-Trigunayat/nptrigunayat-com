By using this website, submitting the contact form, or engaging our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Effective Date: January 5, 2026  ·  Website: https://nptrigunayat.com
Section 01

Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Voluntarily Provide

We collect information you directly provide when you fill out forms, request a quote, or contact us. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Project Details: Selected service plan (e.g., "Starter," "Business"), budget, and specific project requirements or messages you enter in the text fields.
  • Communication: Any correspondence sent via email or the contact form.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the website, we may automatically collect certain technical data, including:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type (mobile/desktop).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources.
  • Technical Logs: IP address and connection logs for security and performance monitoring.
Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for legitimate business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To assess your project requirements, provide quotes, and deliver web development services.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send proposals, and provide project updates.
  • Administrative: To process invoices, manage records, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Security: To protect the website from spam, abuse, and security threats.
Section 03

Third-Party Services & Data Processors

We utilize trusted third-party service providers to operate specific website functions. These providers have their own privacy policies.

3.1. EmailJS (Form Processing)

  • Function: We use EmailJS to process and deliver the inquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • Data Shared: Name, email, phone number, and message content entered in the form.
  • Purpose: To securely transmit your message to our inbox and send you an automated confirmation receipt.
  • Privacy Policy: EmailJS Privacy Policy

3.2. Hosting & Infrastructure

  • Function: Our website is hosted on third-party cloud infrastructure (e.g., Netlify/Vercel/GitHub Pages).
  • Data Shared: IP addresses and access logs.
  • Purpose: To ensure the website is accessible, secure, and fast.

3.3. External Links

  • Social Media: The website contains links to social media platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, GitHub). Clicking these links may allow those platforms to collect data about you.
  • WhatsApp: The "WhatsApp" button redirects you to the WhatsApp application. Your interaction with WhatsApp is governed by Meta's privacy policy.
Section 04

Cookies and Tracking

  • Functional Cookies: The website may use local storage or session cookies to enhance user experience (e.g., remembering scroll position or animation states).
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or invasive tracking pixels (such as Facebook Pixel) on this website.
  • Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting the core functionality of the website.
Section 05

Data Retention & Security

  • Security: We implement standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption, to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Retention: We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected (e.g., completing a project) or to comply with legal, tax, or accounting requirements.
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
Section 06

Your Rights

Under applicable Indian laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for future processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@nptrigunayat.com.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.
